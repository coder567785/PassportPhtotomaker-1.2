Why I Built This

Hi, I'm Amol. I'm a humanities student based in New Delhi, and like many people, I once had to rush to a photo studio just to get a passport photo made — only to pay ₹300 for something that took the photographer 5 minutes.

That frustrated me. The internet already has all the tools needed to do this for free. So I decided to build PassSnap — a completely free, privacy-first passport and profile photo maker that runs entirely in your browser.

No server, no account, no fee. Just upload your photo, pick your size, and download. That's it.

"My mission is simple: make a professional-quality passport photo tool that every student, traveler, and job seeker in India — and the world — can use for free, forever."
